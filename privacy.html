Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Remindial ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Remindial mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Phone Number: Collected during account registration for authentication (OTP verification) and to send SMS reminders and notifications.
  • Medication Data: Medication names, dosages, schedules, and adherence logs that you voluntarily enter into the App. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Ride & Appointment Data: Pickup/dropoff locations, times, and calendar events that you create or sync from your device calendar.
  • Voice Recordings: Optional voice clips you record for personalized reminders. These are stored locally on your device.
  • Contact Information: Names and phone numbers of family members, partners, or caregivers that you voluntarily provide for shared notifications.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and locale settings for app functionality and language detection.
  • Calendar Data: With your explicit permission, we read events from your device calendar to provide appointment reminders. We do not modify or delete your calendar events.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send medication reminders, ride alerts, and appointment notifications via push notifications and SMS.
  • Authenticate your account using one-time passwords (OTP) sent via SMS.
  • Send SMS notifications to family members or caregivers you have designated in the App.
  • Provide text-to-speech (TTS) voice reminders in your selected language.
  • Track medication adherence and provide smart rescheduling suggestions.
  • Send refill alerts when your medication supply is running low.
  • Improve and optimize the App's functionality and user experience.

3. SMS & Communications

Remindial sends transactional SMS messages only. These include:

  • One-time passwords (OTP) for account verification.
  • Medication reminders at your scheduled times.
  • Ride pickup/dropoff alerts with navigation links.
  • Appointment reminders synced from your calendar.
  • Medication adherence confirmations to designated family members.
  • Escalation alerts when medication doses are missed.

We do not send unsolicited marketing messages. You opt-in to SMS by providing your phone number in the App and enabling SMS notifications for specific reminders. You may opt-out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message or by disabling SMS notifications within the App. Standard message and data rates may apply.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your health and personal data is primarily stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (AsyncStorage). We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For user authentication (phone number and session data) and SMS scheduling. Supabase uses industry-standard encryption and is hosted on AWS infrastructure.
  • Twilio: For sending SMS messages and OTP codes. Twilio is SOC 2 compliant and encrypts data in transit.
  • Google Places API: For location search when setting up ride reminders. Location queries are sent to Google but are not stored by us.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your designated contacts: When you assign a reminder to a family member, partner, or caregiver and enable SMS notifications, we send reminder-related SMS messages to the phone numbers you provide.
  • Service providers: We use Supabase and Twilio as described above to operate the App's core functionality.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access & Correction: You can view and edit all your data directly within the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual reminders, medications, or your entire account at any time. Deleting your account removes all associated data from our servers.
  • Opt-out of SMS: Reply STOP to any SMS message or disable SMS notifications in the App settings.
  • Calendar Access: You can revoke calendar permission at any time through your device settings.
  • Notification Control: You can manage push notification preferences through your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

8. International Data Transfers

If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers and service providers are located. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
